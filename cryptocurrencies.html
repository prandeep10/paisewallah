About Crypto-Currencies

Crypto-Currency is by name a Secret Currency, but as you all know, it is no more a secret. It is a digital currency which is independent of any bank or government. So, who manage this currencies? It is regulated by some high-power computers known as miners. This miners work on the block-chain method.

How it works

Whenever a transaction happens it passes by this computers and they have to solve an equation to pass it to the next computer. This equations are solved within seconds and as it is done in encoded forms, as it as done in encoded form our identity is hidden in the process, if any computer is disturbed in the process it will disturb the entire chain and all other computers will get to know it. We can trade in Crypto-Currencies, To buy and sell Crypto-Currencies we need a Broker application, In India we have apps like CoinDCX. There are more than 2000 different Crypto-Currencies, Out of which some popular ones are Bitcoin, Dogecoin, Ethereum, etc. In each Crypto-Currency, a limited number of coins are generated. Like there are only 21-million Bitcoins in the world making it rare. It's value depends on its demand, if more people buys, price will increase. Price of 1-Bitcoin as of 18 October, 2021 is 48 lakhs.

Trading Strategy

Trick is simple, you have to buy in less price and sell it in more price. Like in 2011 the value of 1 Bitcoin was only ₹75, imagine if you would have bought 10 Bitcoin in ₹750 in 2011 and sold it today in 4 Crores. This is still possible, what you have to do is wait for value of Bitcoin to go down, it won’t go down to ₹75, but as you got the point, wait for it to touch the peak-value (highest point) in downward, buy it that time and sell it when it touch the peak value in upward.

Peak Value

Sometimes we cannot exactly predict the peak value and it’s okay atleast we will earn some profit by selling it early and hopefully we will not make the same mistake again, also while waiting for it to rise to peak value we may not even realize it and it may touch the peak value and go down again. With experience we will be able to predict the market more precisely.
